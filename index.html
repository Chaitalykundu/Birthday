🎉 Happy Birthday Love ❤️

We started as just colleagues...

sharing office gossip, random complaints, silly talks about work and people.

And somewhere between those conversations, we started sharing our days... our thoughts... our little problems... and eventually everything.

Just two months ago, we became closer than I ever expected.

People told me different things about you... they said you had ego, they said a lot of things.

But the person I found was completely different — someone with a soft heart, someone who cares deeply, someone who makes me feel special in ways words can't explain.

Yes, sometimes you disappear and make me miss you a little too much... but somehow your care always finds its way back to me.

And the funniest part? We still haven’t met yet...

But I’m already waiting for that day so eagerly — the day I’ll finally see you standing in front of me.

Until then... thank you for becoming such a beautiful part of my life.

Happy Birthday to the person who unexpectedly became my favorite chapter ❤️
